This project shows results regarding the simulation of the physical characteristics of dielectrophoresis, which focused on the modeling of the electric field generated by two plates of different charges and a polarized particle.

This was done with the purpose of studying the characteristics of this non-uniform field, for use when representing Visually modeling malaria detection through dielectrophoresis.

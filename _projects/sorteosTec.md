On this project, I worked on different areas:

- the architecture of our database and the definition of our views needed for our dashboard.
- creation of one of our games, Borrego Tower, in Unity with C#.
- visual design, creating most of the images for our games in an 8-bit art style using Photoshop.
- making our dashboard using my experience using Looker Studio.
